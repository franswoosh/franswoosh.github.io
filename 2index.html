Light of the Morning

When dawn awakens the slumbered skies, His mercy greets my weary eyes. Each ray a whisper of His grace, A gentle touch, a warm embrace.

Quiet Waters

He leads me through the silent stream, A path of peace, a sacred dream. In stillness, I can hear Him say, "Child, walk with Me along the way."
